=====================================================
SGAF positions itself as a professional, modern, full-service

Their key promise is that they manage the financial side so clients 
can focus on operating and expanding their companies.

PRIMARY MESSAGE:
- They serve as an outsourced finance department.
- They handle accounting, tax, payroll, and financial reporting.
- They emphasize clarity, confidence, and expert guidance.

KEY VALUE PROPOSITIONS:
- Full-service accounting and advisory combined in one firm.
- Suitable for businesses generating roughly $1M–$20M in revenue.
- Specialized across several industries with tailored solutions.
- Data-driven reporting and KPI insights.
- Digital, remote, and scalable operations.

CREDIBILITY POINTS:
- Over 1,800 active clients.
- More than 50 professionals in the organization.
- Nationally recognized, with mentions of high-growth lists.
- Known for high client satisfaction and referral-driven growth.

CORE CTA:
- “Talk to an Advisor” or “Schedule a Call” with a guided intake form.

2. SERVICES OVERVIEW
=====================================================
SGAF groups its offerings under three major departments:

A. CLIENT ACCOUNTING SERVICES (CAS)
B. TAX SERVICES
C. ADVISORY SERVICES

Each service line supports recurring monthly operations and long-term 
business decision-making.

=====================================================
2A. CLIENT ACCOUNTING SERVICES (CAS)
=====================================================
This is the backbone of SGAF’s business, offering businesses a 
consistent, accurate monthly accounting workflow.

CAS COMPONENTS:
- Bookkeeping & Monthly Close
- Accounts Payable & Accounts Receivable oversight
- Payroll processing & compliance
- Financial statement preparation
- Monthly reporting package
- KPI dashboards for industry-specific metrics
- Cash flow overviews
- Support for external CPA needs or lender requirements

CAS BENEFITS:
- Clean, reliable books delivered every month.
- Smooth operational workflows for clients.
- Reduces administrative burden on business owners.
- Improves decision-making with timely financial data.

CAS METRICS SGAF HIGHLIGHTS:
- On-time monthly close rate.
- Accuracy and clarity in financial packages.
- High reduction in client workload and accounting errors.

=====================================================

=====================================================
SGAF handles business and individual tax needs, with specialization

TAX OFFERINGS:
